Secondary School ETZ Program

Program Highlights

    The program provides an opportunity for Sreenidhi International School students in middle and high school to receive intensive professional coaching and equitable opportunities to participate at high level in various tournaments within India and beyond.

    We encourage all students to participate in sports or a physical activity with the theory that physical exertion contributes to a healthy lifestyle and complements academic work. As Sreenidhians we believe that sports play a pivotal role in developing balanced students, hence, we place a strong focus on our sports programming, encouraging all students to participate in the team sports we offer.

    ETZ Activity sign-up is done online.

    Online sign-up for ETZ 2021-2022 activities will open on Monday, July 22 at 6:00pm and closes on Wednesday, July @ 7:30pm. Before and after these times the on-line sign-up form will be closed.

    August 5 - ETZ program starts.

    March 13 - ETZ program ends.

Conferences

Sreenidhi International School students are involved in a range of Athletic conferences.

These include :

  • ISBL - Indian School Basketball League
  • SGFI - School Games Federation of India
  • Jr NBA - Junior NBA Regional and National Championship
  • IBSO - International Board Schools Sports Organization
  • GHIBL - Greater Hyderabad Inter-school basketball league
  • Premier Cup - This a football tournament organized by Sreenidhi Football Club
  • SFL - School Football League
  • Bengal Junior Squash Open - Kolkata
  • Northern Indian Squash Tournament - Delhi
  • All India Rajasthan Squash Championship - Jodhpur
  • CCI Western India Squash Tournament - Mumbai
  • South Zone Aquatic Championship
  • Hyderabad Cricket Association League
  • School Cricket League
  • VVS cricket Tournament
  • Inter-District State Athletics Championship
  • All India Tennis Association ranking tournaments (AITA)

Coaching Fee Payment Process

STEP 1
Go to Google or IOS play store and download the Sreenidhi Sports Academy APP.

STEP 2
To sign in, after receiving a registration confirmation; Enter your registered mail ID and set your password. It will direct you to your ward's portal.

To pay the coaching fees, click "Payments" and follow the process. It will direct you to a payment gateway for details. Once you have made the payment successfully, you have officially registered your ward into the sports program with Sreenidhi Sports Academy.
